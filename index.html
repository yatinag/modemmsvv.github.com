About Modem

The event connects the following values

Evolving Abilities

The first thing us Modernites are taught is to be sharp, and to the point. Modem is just another way of challenging these qualities, exposing students to the way of Modern and having a great experience.

Better Society

Through Modem we aim at building a better community, a better society and a better world my making our future generations more aware through practical applications of the skills they know about.

Evolving Tech

Technology stumps the last update over and over again, and perfection seems like a myth. That is just how nature works. You must know there where the finish line is, for otherwise where would you steer your ship(of knowledge).

The Quintessential Quizzer

Result

Winning Team: Delhi Public School R. K. Puram
Runner's up Team: Mount St. Mary's School


Is it a general knowledge quiz? Or, maybe, a technology based quiz? Maybe it’s a science fiction quiz? But really it’s all of them and much more. So, come prepared for anything. An amalgam of current affairs, relevant dates, organisations, policies, terms, media and inventions which could relate to the huge spectrum of computer science. The topic and questions would be declared on the spot. May the quirkiest, quintessential quizzer win!

The Desktop Designer

Result

Winning Team: Modern School, Barakhamba Road
Runner's up Team: Gyan Bharti School, Saket


The desktop designer event looks for artists who use the computer as their main tool. Instead of finely-crafted brushes, the desktop designer prefers to use the mighty pen tool. Instead of a selection of 24 colours, the desktop designer has access to 16.7 million. A bunch of LEDs make up the desktop designer’s canvas. A deep understanding of photoshop is required, be prepared to adapt to anything! A true test of creativity awaits you.

Software provided: Adobe Photoshop CS5
No. of students per team: 1
Participants from grades: 9th-12th
